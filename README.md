### 🧾 AI Legal Contract Analyzer — *RAG over legal documents*
Conversational analysis platform that turns dense legal contracts into clear, grounded answers.
Users upload a document and ask questions; responses are retrieved and grounded to avoid hallucination.

`Python` · `Groq (Mixtral) LLM` · `FAISS Vector Search` · `LangChain Agents` · `RAG`

**Highlight:** end-to-end retrieval-augmented pipeline (embeddings → FAISS retrieval → LLM answer) for reliable, source-grounded Q&A.
🔗 [Repository](https://github.com/indkshitij/AI-Legal-Contract-Analyzer)

---

`React` · `Vapi AI (Voice)` · `Google Gemini` · `Prompt Engineering` · `Schema-validated AI responses`

**Highlight:** real-time AI voice agent + schema-validated feedback (communication, problem-solving, clarity) with a progress dashboard.
🔗 [Live Demo](https://brainwaveai.onrender.com/) · [Repository](https://github.com/indkshitij/BrainwaveAI)

`MERN` · `Google GenAI (Gemini)` · `Clerk` · `Inngest` · `Svix` · `Resend`

**Highlight:** AI receipt parsing + event-driven workflows for automated alerts and monthly financial reports.
